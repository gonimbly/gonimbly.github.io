Engager

A mobile application framework to engage and reward your customers

Brief

Customer success is an art that requires constant customer engagement. This engagement is often done through static tools that don’t reflect the dynamic nature of your business. With the Heroku powered mobile app “Engager” you can create dynamic surveys that offer incentivizes your customers to respond by giving them points to redeem for rewards like discounted Uber rides or Amazon credit.

Imagine you just held a conference and you would like to know what your customers thought of the event. Within the 'Engager' app you could add a new question like, "How much did you enjoy the conference?”, and send a notification to all of your customers. The customers would login to the ‘Engager’ app and respond to the question.

Each question can be incentivized with a unique ‘point’ vale driving your customers to answer the questions are most important first.

Engager uses Heroku connect to get your customer success data into your Salesforce instance where you can build dashboards and reports to take action on your findings.

Now customer success data can be used to empower sales, marketing, support, and finance in ways that seemed impossible before.
